</p><p>Risk: Low
</p><p>Version: Lynx compiled from FreeBSD ports collection. Also tested in 2.8.5dev.5.gz
</p><p>[larryc@harod ~ $] lynx --version<br/>
Lynx Version 2.8.4rel.1 (17 Jul 2001)<br/>
Built on freebsd4.4 Dec 25 2001 23:04:31
</p><p>Details<br/>
line 7995 in LYUtils.c reads:<br/>
syslog (LOG_INFO|LOG_LOCAL5, buf);
</p><p>The reason this is low priority is the bug can only big triggered if sysloging URL&amp;#039;s is enabled.
(./configure --enable-syslog)
