A root owned binary &amp;quot;gunzip&amp;quot; is created in /tmp with world writeable permissions, the pid is appended to the filename.
</p><p>TMP_GUNZIP=$TMPDIR/gunzip$$
</p><p>$ ls -l /tmp/gunzip16152<br/>
--rwxrwxrwx 1 root root 51808 Jan 14 00:15 gunzip16152
</p><p>gunzip is extracted:
</p><pre>
                    extract gunzip &amp;gt; &amp;quot;$TMP_GUNZIP&amp;quot; 2&amp;gt;&amp;gt;$SHXLOGFILE
                    extract gunzip | uncompress &amp;gt; &amp;quot;$TMP_GUNZIP&amp;quot; 2&amp;gt;&amp;gt;$SHXLOGFILE
</pre>

</p><p>chmod 777 $TMP_GUNZIP &amp;gt;/dev/null 2&amp;gt;&amp;amp;1 The binary is used during installation:
